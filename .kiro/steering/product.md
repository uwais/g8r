# Product Overview

E-commerce web application for multi-store catalog management with specialized pharmacy features.

## Core Functionality

**Customer Portal**
- Browse products across multiple stores with real-time stock
- Search and filter by price, category, prescription status
- Compare prices and locations for same products across sellers
- Distance-based sorting from delivery address
- Product and store reviews with ratings
- Shopping cart with delivery/pickup options
- Prescription upload and management for Rx items
- Purchase history and personalized recommendations

**Seller Portal**
- Multi-store inventory management
- Individual product CRUD operations
- Bulk CSV upload for catalog items
- Order management with prescription verification workflow
- Support for both general products and pharmacy-specific items

## Key Features

- Dual authentication system (customer/seller roles)
- Pharmacy-specific fields (drug name, dosage, prescription requirements)
- Geolocation-based distance calculations
- Prescription document upload (.pdf, .jpg, .png)
- Order verification workflow for prescription items
- Store preference marking
- Product grouping by drug name for comparison shopping
